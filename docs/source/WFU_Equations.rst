WFU Equations
=============

Waveform Differences
--------------------
Amplitude difference is defined generally as:

\begin{equation}
\nonumber
    \Delta{A}_{\mu}(f;\theta)= \begin{cases} 
      \frac{|\mu_{IMR}(f;\theta)|-|\mu_{EOB}(f;\theta)|}{|\mu_{IMR}(f;\theta)|} & f \leq f_{COR} \\
      \Delta{A}_{\mu}(f_{COR};\theta) & f > f_{COR} 
   \end{cases}
\end{equation}
